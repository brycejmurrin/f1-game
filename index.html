HOW TO PLAY

PC / KEYBOARD
Steer AD or ←→   Gas W / ↑   Brake S / ↓   Boost Space   Overtake X   Camera C
Shift Up E   Shift Down Q / Shift   Pause P / Esc
TOUCH / MOBILE
Pick STEER in the pause menu: TILT your phone (auto-calibrates at the lights), on-screen BUTTONS, or tap left/right screen HALVES. In button and touch modes the car accelerates for you — just steer and brake
⚡ BOOST
Toggle electric deploy on/off. Drains the energy bar; recharges when you lift or brake. Tapers off at very high speed — 2026 rules
OVERTAKE
Within 1 s of the car ahead the OT light arms. Activate for 4 s of full-power deploy with no taper — the 2026 DRS replacement
GEARS
Toggle GEARS: MANUAL in the pause menu. Shift up near the redline, down for corner exits. The tachometer + gear indicator sit bottom-centre
🎥 CAMERA
Tap the CAM button (top-right) or press C to cycle CHASE → FAR → COCKPIT → HOOD. Your choice is remembered
TRACK LIMITS
Grass is slow. Cutting the track repeatedly earns a +5 s penalty
RACE
22 cars, five red lights, lights out. Points 25-18-15-12-10-8-6-4-2-1
SEASON
All 24 circuits in calendar order — championship standings saved between visits
TIME TRIAL
Solo on track, no rivals. Set your fastest lap; your track record is saved and shown on the circuit list

STEERING & ASSISTS

Pick a preset to set everything at once — most players never need more. Adjust the few controls below to taste, or open Advanced for the full set of knobs. Everything takes effect immediately and is saved.

How sharply the car answers your phone tilt. Higher = less tilt needed for full lock (more sensitive); lower = relaxed, bigger tilts.

STEERING

Overall steering feel, from forgiving and stable to sharp and direct.

DRIVING HELP

How much of each corner the car steers for you. Higher = easier to stay on track.

RACING LINE

Optional gentle pull toward the ideal line. Never overrides your steering.

Pace of the whole field — you and the AI together. Higher = everyone faster; racing stays competitive.

TILT INPUT how phone motion becomes steering (tilt mode)

The same sensitivity as the top slider, with a finer label: how far you tilt the phone for full lock. Higher = less tilt needed; lower = relaxed, big tilts.

How steady the tilt filter holds the wheel. Higher = smoother and calmer at rest (kills hand jitter) with a touch more lag; lower = more immediate.

CAR HANDLING how steering becomes motion (all inputs)

How much of each corner the car steers for you. Higher = the car tracks the bend automatically and you just trim the line; lower = you do the work. The single biggest “easier to drive” knob.

Turn-in snappiness (shorter wheelbase). Higher = sharper, faster turn-in; lower = lazier and more stable.

Input curve. Low = gentle near centre for fine corrections; high = direct, 1:1 response. Raise it if steering feels mushy near centre.

Maximum turn angle at full lock. Higher = tighter cornering, but easier to over-rotate. (Auto-tapers at high speed.)

How much steering you keep at high speed. Higher = sharper and more responsive flat-out; lower = calmer and more stable.

DRIVER AID

Optional bias toward the line. PULL (+) eases toward the apex; PUSH (−) runs wider; OFF = pure manual. Never overrides your steering.

LIGHTING TUNER

Live lighting & rendering values for the loaded track — the scene on the left previews every change instantly. Each track keeps a separate set per time-of-day and weather. COPY VALUES exports this profile as JSON.
