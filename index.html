HOW TO PLAY

STEER
Tilt your phone (calibrates at the lights) — or touch the screen halves / ←→ / A D
THROTTLE
Automatic. BRAKE: ↓ / S / brake button
⚡ BOOST
Hold Space / BOOST to deploy electric power. Drains the energy bar; recharges when you brake or lift. Deploy tapers off at very high speed — 2026 rules
OVERTAKE
Within 1s of the car ahead the OT light arms. Press X / OT for 4s of full-power deploy with no taper — the 2026 DRS replacement
TRACK LIMITS
Grass is slow. Cutting the track repeatedly earns a +5s penalty
RACE
22 cars, five red lights, lights out. Points 25-18-15-12-10-8-6-4-2-1
SEASON
All 12 circuits in calendar order — championship standings saved between visits
