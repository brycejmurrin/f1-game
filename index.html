HOW TO PLAY

STEER
Pick STEER in the pause menu: TILT your phone (calibrates at the lights), on-screen BUTTONS, or TOUCH the left/right of the screen. Keyboard A D always works
THROTTLE
In TILT + MANUAL gears, hold GAS / W / ↑ to accelerate. In AUTO (touch / button steering) the car accelerates for you — just steer and brake. BRAKE: hold BRAKE / S / ↓
⚡ BOOST
Tap BOOST / Space to toggle electric deploy on/off. Drains the energy bar; recharges when you lift or brake. Tapers off at very high speed — 2026 rules
OVERTAKE
Within 1s of the car ahead the OT light arms. Tap OT / X for 4s of full-power deploy with no taper — the 2026 DRS replacement
GEARS
Toggle GEARS: MANUAL in the pause menu. Shift up ▲ / E near the redline, down ▼ / Q for corner exits. The tachometer + gear show bottom-centre; auto mode shifts for you
TRACK LIMITS
Grass is slow. Cutting the track repeatedly earns a +5s penalty
RACE
22 cars, five red lights, lights out. Points 25-18-15-12-10-8-6-4-2-1
SEASON
All 12 circuits in calendar order — championship standings saved between visits
TIME TRIAL
Solo on track, no rivals. Set your fastest lap over four laps; your track record is saved and shown on the circuit list
