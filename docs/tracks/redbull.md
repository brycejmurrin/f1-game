# Circuit Red Bull Ring — Visual Design Brief

**Setting:** DAY, green theme (Styrian alps). ~4.32 km, 10 turns, clockwise. ~65 m elevation change.

## 1. Setting
A short, punchy circuit cut into the green hills of Spielberg, Styria, Austria — over 600 m above sea level, ringed by forested Alpine mountains (≈60% of Styria is forest). Compact loop of long straights and big climbs, with a dramatic alpine backdrop and the giant charging-bull statue watching over the valley.

## 2. Atmosphere & palette
Bright, clear alpine daylight, vivid summer-green hills against forested dark-green mountains and snow-flecked peaks on the far horizon. Optional low valley fog clinging to the bowl at dawn.
- Alpine sky: `[0.52, 0.70, 0.88]` (clean high-altitude blue)
- Vivid green hills (foreground): `[0.36, 0.62, 0.24]`
- Forested mountains (mid/far): `[0.18, 0.34, 0.20]`, hazed: `[0.40, 0.52, 0.46]`
- Distant peaks: `[0.62, 0.68, 0.70]` (grey-blue, snow flecks white)
- Tarmac: `[0.27, 0.28, 0.30]`; green/gravel run-off: `[0.40, 0.55, 0.30]`
- Kerbs: red `[0.80, 0.14, 0.14]` / white `[0.90, 0.90, 0.90]`
- Red Bull brand red/navy: `[0.82, 0.10, 0.16]` / `[0.10, 0.14, 0.40]`

## 3. Elevation
Big ~65 m swing per lap; the bowl makes climbs and drops very readable.
- s≈0.00–0.10: launch uphill off the start straight, **steep climb** into Turn 1 (Niki Lauda Kurve).
- s≈0.12–0.25: keep climbing to the **high point at Turn 3 (Remus)** — circuit's crest.
- s≈0.25–0.55: **long descent** through Turn 4 (Schlossgold) and fast downhill sweepers.
- s≈0.70–1.00: undulating final sector dropping back down into the stadium / start straight.

## 5. Track features
- Steep gradients — exaggerate the climb to T1/T3 and the long drop after.
- Fast uphill corners (T1, T3) taken at speed despite the gradient.
- Heavy braking zones into T1, T3, and T4 — wide run-off, big kerbs.
- Bold red/white kerbs with sausage-kerb box bumps on apexes and exits.

## 6. Modelling notes
- Make elevation the hero: tilt and raise the ground boxes so the climb to T3 and the descent read clearly from the camera.
- Layer the horizon — vivid green foreground hills, dark-green forested mid-ridges, grey-blue snow-flecked peaks behind, each haze-faded further back.
- Plant the dark bull-under-arch box on a green hillside above the lower sector; keep it oversized and silhouette-readable.
- Tint grandstands and gantry banners Red Bull red/navy; concentrate stands at the start, T3 crest, and the final stadium bowl.
- Scatter tall narrow cone-boxes as pine forest along the descending mid-sector; add a long smooth-green ski-jump-style ramp box on one far hillside.
- Use green-tinted run-off pans rather than grey asphalt aprons to keep the alpine-green theme dominant.
